Frequently Asked Questions

What do the rounding options mean?

No Rounding: Shows exact division with all decimal places. Round Down: Rounds each person's share down to nearest cent, then adds leftover cents to first few people. Round Up: Rounds each person's share up to nearest cent, then subtracts excess from last few people.

Do you store my data?

No! Everything runs in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your bill information. Your privacy is protected.
